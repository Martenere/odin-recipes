Recipe for wheat worms in tasty liquid

A bowl of the final product, put some beauty on top to increase satisfaction

This recipe of noodles is suitable for the following occacions

  • if you are deprived of NaCl
  • can't muster the strength to chew your food
  • Medium family gathering

Ingredients

  • Wheat noodles
  • Broth - cooked on either concious or unconcious items
  • Garnish - preferably green

Conjuring stage

  1. Boil wheat noodles in broth
  2. Pour result into a bowl
  3. Add green beauty
  4. Eat
